よくある質問 — Before you come

How long is dinner?

About two hours at the counter, a little less at a table. We recommend leaving the evening open — it is not a meal to rush.

Can you handle allergies and diets?

With notice, yes. Tell us when you reserve and the chef composes toward you. We cannot guarantee a nut-free or shellfish-free kitchen, but pescatarian and most allergy needs are easily met. The omakase is not vegetarian by nature; a vegetable-forward counter is offered with 48 hours' notice.

Is there a dress code?

None. Come as you are comfortable. The room is warm and unfussy.

Can I bring children?

Guests aged 12 and over are welcome at the counter. The pace and seating suit older diners best.

Do you allow photos?

A quiet photo of a course is fine. We ask that phones otherwise rest — the counter is close, and the evening is better watched than recorded.
